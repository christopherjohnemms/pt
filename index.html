Here at Podge & Tin we serve awesome food, using and supporting our local independents for our fine meats and fresh fish.

There is something for everyone including fresh burgers, steaks, very peri chicken, wraps, pittas, salads and also our famous fish & chips.

We also have an amazing range of delicious milkshakes along with our fully licensed bar serving world beers.
